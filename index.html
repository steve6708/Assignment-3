Concepts Learned in Chemistry

  • Periodic Table (元素周期表): The periodic table is a chart that organizes all known elements by their atomic number, electron configurations, and recurring chemical properties.
  • Properties of Chemical Reactions (化学反应的性质): These include the characteristics of reactants and products, energy changes, and reaction rates.
  • Factors Affecting Reaction Rates (影响反应速率的因素): These include temperature, concentration, surface area, and the presence of catalysts.
  • Chemical Kinetics (化学动力学): The study of the rates of chemical processes and the factors affecting them.
  • Chemical Equilibrium (化学平衡): A state in which both reactants and products are present in concentrations which have no further tendency to change with time.
  • Acids and Bases (酸和碱): Substances that can donate protons (H+) and accept electrons (bases), respectively.
  • pH and pOH (酸碱度和碱度): pH is a measure of the hydrogen ion concentration, while pOH is a measure of the hydroxide ion concentration.
  • Hydrolysis (水解): A chemical process in which a molecule of water is added to a substance.
  • Electrolysis (电解): A technique that uses a direct electric current to drive a non-spontaneous chemical reaction.
  • Atomic Structure (原子结构): The composition and arrangement of protons, neutrons, and electrons in an atom.
  • Electron Configuration (电子排布): The distribution of electrons of an atom or molecule in atomic or molecular orbitals.
  • Bonding (化学键合): The joining of two atoms in a stable arrangement. This includes ionic, covalent, and metallic bonds.
  • Stoichiometry (化学计量学): The calculation of reactants and products in chemical reactions.
  • Gas Laws (气体定律): The laws that describe the behavior of gases, including Boyle's law, Charles's law, and Avogadro's law.
  • Thermochemistry (热化学): The study of the energy and heat associated with chemical reactions and physical transformations.
  • Redox Reactions (氧化还原反应): Reactions that involve the transfer of electrons between two species.
  • Le Chatelier's Principle (勒夏特列原理): The principle stating that if a system at equilibrium is subjected to a disturbance, it will adjust itself to counteract the disturbance and restore equilibrium.
  • Entropy (熵): A measure of the disorder or randomness in a system.
  • Enthalpy (焓): The total heat content of a system.
  • Gibbs Free Energy (吉布斯自由能): The energy associated with a chemical reaction that can be used to do work.

Practical Applications Learned Through the Course

In the course, the teacher often asked us to think about the practical applications of these theoretical knowledge, such as rust removal by electrolysis, batteries, water purification, biodegradable plastics, and fuel cells. These theoretical applications are mainly related to drug synthesis, environmental science, industrial production, food chemistry, materials science, and energy production.

在课程中，老师经常让我们去思考这些理论知识的现实应用，比如电解除锈、电池、水净化、生物降解塑料和燃料电池等。这些理论的现实应用主要涉及药物的合成、环境科学、工业生产、食品化学、材料科学和能源生产等。

Drug Synthesis (药物合成)

Amoxicillin, with the chemical formula C16H19N3O5S, is a commonly synthesized antibiotic. Another example is Ibuprofen, with the chemical formula C13H18O2, which is a widely used pain reliever.

阿莫西林，化学分子式为C16H19N3O5S，是一种常见的合成抗生素。另一个例子是布洛芬，化学分子式为C13H18O2，是一种广泛使用的止痛药。

Environmental Science (环境科学)

pH balance is crucial in agricultural production. Proper pH levels in soil ensure optimal nutrient availability for crops. Additionally, understanding redox reactions helps in the treatment of wastewater.

酸碱平衡在农业生产中至关重要。土壤中的适当pH水平确保作物的最佳养分供应。此外，了解氧化还原反应有助于废水处理。

Industrial Processes (工业生产)

Chemical kinetics and thermochemistry are used to optimize industrial processes such as the production of fertilizers and refining of petroleum. This helps in increasing reaction speeds and yields while reducing energy consumption.

化学动力学和热化学用于优化工业生产过程，如化肥生产和石油精炼。这有助于提高反应速度和产量，同时降低能耗。

Food Chemistry (食品化学)

Preservatives like sodium benzoate (C7H5NaO2) help extend the shelf life of foods. Understanding chemical reactions in food can improve processing and preservation methods, enhancing nutritional value and safety.

防腐剂如苯甲酸钠（C7H5NaO2）有助于延长食品的保质期。了解食品中的化学反应可以改进加工和保存方法，增强营养价值和安全性。

Materials Science (材料科学)

Developing new materials like high-strength alloys, semiconductors, and nanomaterials involves understanding chemical bonding and electron configurations. These materials have applications in electronics, construction, and aerospace industries.

开发新材料如高强度合金、半导体和纳米材料涉及了解化学键和电子排布。这些材料在电子产品、建筑和航空航天工业中有广泛应用。

Energy Production (能源生产)

Fuel cells and solar panels are examples of energy technologies that rely on understanding electrolysis and redox reactions. Hydrogen fuel cells (using reactions like 2H2 + O2 → 2H2O) offer a clean energy source.

燃料电池和太阳能电池板是依赖于了解电解和氧化还原反应的能源技术示例。氢燃料电池（使用反应如2H2 + O2 → 2H2O）提供了一种清洁的能源来源。
